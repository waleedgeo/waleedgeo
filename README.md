Mirza Waleed is a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Geography at [Hong Kong Baptist University](https://www.hkbu.edu.hk/), specializing in **geospatial data analytics**, **cloud computing (Google Earth Engine)**, and **AI-driven modeling** for **flood hazard management**. As a [Google Developer Expert in Earth Engine](https://g.dev/waleedgeo) and visiting scholar at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST), he applies high-performance platforms like [Shaheen](https://www.hpc.kaust.edu.sa/) and [Google Earth Engine](https://earthengine.google.com/) to advance flood risk assessment and environmental monitoring. His work integrates remote sensing, machine learning, and big data analytics to develop innovative, data-driven solutions that contribute to resilient, informed disaster management. With a focus on urbanization impacts, climate resilience, and predictive modeling, Waleed is dedicated to enhancing geospatial science and fostering community resilience through cutting-edge research.
